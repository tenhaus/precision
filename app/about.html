PRECISION CONCRETE

Precision Concrete Construction Company, Inc. is a regional company that provides high quality, economical products to our customers every day with a focus on the future. We work to achieve mutual success for our company and our customers by building relationships, thinking progressively, and communicating openly on all levels. We have many industry certifications and qualifications. Our certifications include:

  • American Concrete Institute: Concrete Field Testing Technician – Grade 1 & 2
  • American Concrete Institute: Concrete Flatwork Finisher & Technician
  • American Concrete Institute: Concrete Flatwork Technician
  • American Concrete Institute: Concrete Flatwork Commercial & Industrial Finisher
  • American Concrete Institute: Concrete Flatwork High Tolerance Floors
  • American Concrete Institute: Tilt-Up Supervisor
  • American Concrete Institute: Tilt-Up Certified
  • FACE : Floor Flatness and Levelness Testing

  • ASSOCIATIONS

    PCC is a member of a number of associations. Our participation provides us with opportunities to continue to learn and grow within the industry. PCC has been a member of the Tilt-Up Concrete Association since 1987.

  • GIVING BACK

    We are committed to giving back to our community. We participate in several charitable projects with non-profit organizations in our community. If you would like to work with PCC on a charitable project, contact us at 410-256-2800.

  • AWARDS

    We have received various awards and honorable mentions over the years including the Golden Trowel Award for the flattest and most level floor slab placed in the world in a year, and six Tilt Up Achievement awards since 2005, including one Excellence in Achievement

SAFETY AT PPC

We are committed to construction safety and we make that commitment our top priority. An accident on the job site takes a heavy emotional and financial toll on everyone involved. At PCC, we are very proud of our comprehensive safety program.

The cornerstone of our safety program is the on-going training of supervisors, foremen, operators, drivers and other tradespeople to practice safety awareness. Our safety program ensures that all employees have the proper skills needed to work safely including OSHA 30 and OSHA 10 Hour Training Certificates.

Our full-time Safety Director strives to ensure that every employee is protected from injury each day they come into work. The Safety Director regulates all projects by OSHA standards. PCC has an excellent safety record.

PCC also conducts job site visits and inspections, which are integral parts of our safety program. All of these components combined help PCC manage the risks associated with job site accidents.

Substance abuse testing

Any prospective employee applying for a job at PCC is required to complete a drug screening. Applicants who test positive for illegal drugs are immediately disqualified. Any PCC employee who is involved in an accident or injury on the job is subject to additional screening for illegal substances. If the employee tests positive for illegal substances, their employment will be terminated.

PCC works on government projects that require background checks and security clearances for employees. Testing positive for illegal substances will compromise the ability of employees to obtain clearances.

Lastly, PCC is compliant with Maryland’s Crane Safety Regulations. The Maryland Commissioner of Labor and Industry has deemed crane operators, signal persons, riggers, and crane operator trainees as safety sensitive positions. For this reason, any employee who works in a safety sensitive position at PCC can be selected at random to be screened for drugs and alcohol.
