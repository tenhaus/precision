OUR SERVICES

We offer a comprehensive list of services. We specialize in tilt-up walls, superflat floors, and various foundations for commercial, government and institutional industries, among others. We provide our customers with progressive and sustainable concrete solutions.

  • FLOORING

    Superflat floors are the industry standard in today’s warehouses and distribution centers. Precision Concrete has been recognized as having built the flattest floor in the world. We know Superflat floors.

  • TILT-UP WALLS

    Tilt-Up construction can offer earlier occupancy, large interior spaces, beauty, durability, and lower operations costs. Precision Concrete is the clear leader in the Mid-Atlantic for tilt-up construction in all industries.

  • FOUNDATIONS

    Foundation work is step one. Precision Concrete Construction has the manpower and experience to work in multiple locations on the same jobsite under even the worst conditions to get you out of the ground quickly.
